About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the community that makes Grace Community Church special

Our Story

Grace Community Church was founded in 1985 with a vision to create a welcoming community where people could encounter God's grace and grow in their faith journey.

What started as a small gathering in a home has grown into a vibrant community of believers from diverse backgrounds, united by our love for Christ and commitment to serving our community.

Over the years, we've remained committed to our core values of faith, hope, and love, continually adapting to meet the needs of our growing congregation while staying true to biblical principles.

Our Journey Since 1985

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God passionately, serve others compassionately, and share the Gospel courageously in our community and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, multi-generational church that transforms lives through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach, impacting Thika and beyond for Christ.

Our Core Values

Biblical Foundation

We are committed to the authority of Scripture and teaching God's Word with clarity and relevance.

Authentic Community

We value genuine relationships where people can be real, find support, and grow together in faith.

Compassionate Service

We demonstrate Christ's love through practical service to our church, community, and world.

Spiritual Growth

We are committed to helping every believer mature in their faith and discover their God-given purpose.

Cultural Relevance

We communicate the timeless truth of the Gospel in ways that connect with contemporary culture.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do as an expression of our worship to God.

What to Expect

Welcoming Community

You'll be greeted by friendly faces and find a place where you can belong.

Inspiring Worship

Engage in meaningful worship that connects you with God through music and prayer.

Biblical Teaching

Hear practical messages from the Bible that apply to your everyday life.
